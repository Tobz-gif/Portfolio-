About Me

I am a 500-level medical student with a passion for health communication and evidence-based writing. My focus is preventive medicine — helping people understand their health before problems arise.

I enjoy translating complex medical concepts into clear, engaging content for two audiences: clinical peers who need precision, and the general public who need clarity. Whether it is a journal-style article or a community health post, I write to inform and empower.

Area of Interest: Preventive Medicine, with a particular focus on health literacy, early disease detection, maternal and neonatal health, and lifestyle-based interventions. I believe that informed communities make healthier choices, and I aim to bridge that gap through writing and outreach.
